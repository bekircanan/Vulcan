Who are we?

We are ambition, we are cooperation, we are resilience.
We are a community dedicated to the mental well-being of young people, together we strive to create a nurturing environment where every individual can thrive, learn, and overcome challenges. We are Vulcan.

We believe in investment and we think that setting up a mandatory time in the standard student's schedule for club activity can make a huge difference. We believe in the power of will and those clubs are created by the students to make sure that everyone can allocate time to a passion, sport, or whatever is important. Our mission is to give power to students by giving them the keys to the door of their life.

Clubs for students, by students.

The problem

The current educational system is not designed to cater to the holistic development of students. The focus on academic excellence often leads to a neglect of mental health and personal growth. This has resulted in a rise in mental health issues among young individuals, including stress, anxiety, and depression. The lack of a balanced educational experience has also hindered the development of essential life skills such as teamwork, communication, and leadership.

What we do?

Join us in advocating for the integration of 2-4 hours dedicated to student-run clubs into the academic schedule, fostering holistic development and mitigating mental health issues among young individuals. Together, let's pave the way for a brighter, more balanced educational experience.
